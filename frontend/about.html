WELCOME TO OUR CHURCH

KING OF GLORY CHRISTIAN FELLOWSHIP

At KGCF, We preach Christ to bring Comfort to souls for a renewed hope that gives Confidence to approach the throne of grace for mercy.

KGCF AS AN UMBRELLA FOR GOD'S PEOPLE

  • KGCF shall serve as an umbrella to all worshippers of Jesus Christ from all nations who shares our beliefs, vision and mission.
  • Members shall fully embrace the golden rule "Do unto others as you would have them do unto you" - Matthew 7:12.

STRATEGIC MINISTRY

  • Building a christian community of people through teaching and training of every christlike principle to enhance personal growth.
  • Finding or recognizing one's gift or talent, developing them to allow for improvement and creating space for those who already have them to express/improve them.
  • Empowerment for the work of the kingdom.

WHAT KGCF IS NOT

  • KGCF is not a social organisation, a club, secular gathering or worldly association or meeting.
  • KGCF is not a political organisation and does not engage in any politics or expression of any political views and has no political affiliations.
  • KGCF shall not allow any activity group, unit or department to be used as a breeding ground for worldliness nor is any allowed to work against the beliefs system of the church.
